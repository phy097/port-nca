About the Exhibition

Fragmentation explores the lived realities of school teachers involved in Myanmar’s Civil Disobedience Movement (CDM) who are now displaced and living in Internally Displaced Persons (IDP) camps in Karenni State. Through documentary photography, the exhibition reflects on loss, resilience, and resistance, revealing how education and hope persist even under conditions of extreme hardship.The exhibition was held from 4 to 16 March 2025 at the Curved Wall, 5th Floor, Bangkok Art and Culture Centre (BACC).
