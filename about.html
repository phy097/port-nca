About Me

I am Nyein Chan Aung. I am a professional documentary photographer from Myanmar, currently based in Karenni State and southern Shan State. My work focuses on documenting frontline realities, the daily lives of resistance fighters, and the humanitarian consequences of armed conflict following the 2021 military coup. Through long-term work in high-risk environments, I also document the lived experiences of internally displaced civilians and collect visual evidence of war crimes committed against civilian populations. In March 2025, I presented my first solo exhibition, Fragments, at the Bangkok Art and Culture Centre, sharing stories from conflict-affected communities in southern Shan State.
